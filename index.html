About Us

Charity: water is a nonprofit dedicated to ending the global water crisis by bringing clean, safe drinking water to people in need. Since 2006, we’ve funded over 186,000 projects in 29 countries, transforming the lives of more than 20 million people. We work with local partners to build sustainable, community-owned water solutions, and every public donation goes directly to the field. Clean water changes everything—improving health, education, equality, and opportunity—and we’re committed to making that change for everyone, everywhere.
